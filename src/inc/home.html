For Allen LeVines, music is in every place at every moment -- in scatterings of morning sun or night shadows, thundering spring waterfalls or silent winter snows, prancing unbridled horses or petals drooping on dew-soaked wildflowers. Music is natural and sacred, physical and metaphysical, ravishing and contemplative.

LeVines is a musician inspired by a global vision, embracing the vast and compelling array of sounds every culture world-wide has to offer. A seasoned composer, pianist, and conductor, his compositions have been described as "atmospheric", his jazz piano as "authentic", and his artistic direction as "illuminating". LeVines’ ongoing multi-cultural studies include Japanese gagaku, West African drumming, Andean flute traditions, and more.
